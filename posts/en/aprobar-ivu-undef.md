# How to pass the IVU Course

> 📚 The [**Introduction to University Life**](https://campus.fadena.undef.edu.ar/course/view.php?id=534) course is part of the **FADENA Entrance**. Passing it with **80% or more** is an **indispensable requirement to be able to enroll in the semester-based subjects** of the Degree in Cyberdefense.

![Introduction to University Life Notice](/announcement-ivu-1.png)

## Quick Guide

In order to take the **Final Integrative Activity** on Monday 3/9, it is mandatory to complete the **questionnaires** for each module:

- **[Intro to University Life Module 1 Questionnaire](https://campus.fadena.undef.edu.ar/mod/quiz/view.php?id=27450)**
- **[Intro to University Life Module 2 Questionnaire](https://campus.fadena.undef.edu.ar/mod/quiz/view.php?id=27452)**
- **[Intro to University Life Module 3 Questionnaire](https://campus.fadena.undef.edu.ar/mod/quiz/view.php?id=27454)**

Once completed, don't forget to register:
- **[Registration for the IVU Integrative Activity shift](https://campus.fadena.undef.edu.ar/mod/choice/view.php?id=27815)**

> 💡 **Tip:** The exercises in the Integrative Activity will be similar to those seen in the questionnaires of each module.

> 📜 **Recommendation:** Read the [**UNDEF Regulations**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1ebdZ_zecr452v2XYp6N1pJkgm6h36EEb/view?usp=sharing) carefully, as several answers will be there.
